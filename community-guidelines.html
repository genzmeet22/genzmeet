GenzMeet - Community Guidelines

Community Guidelines

Welcome to GenzMeet! At GenzMeet, we strive to create a safe, inclusive, and welcoming environment where you can be yourself and make genuine connections. By following these community guidelines, you help ensure a positive and respectful experience for all users.

  1. Respect Boundaries
    Everyone has different comfort levels. Avoid sharing nudity, explicit content, or sexual material on your public profile.
  2. Protect Personal Information
    Be thoughtful when sharing personal details. Do not post sensitive information such as phone numbers, email addresses, or social media handles publicly. Never share or request financial details.
  3. Avoid Violence
    We do not tolerate any violent or threatening content, including images or discussions of harm. Any suggestion of danger may prompt our team to take immediate safety action.
  4. Personal, Not Business
    GenzMeet is for personal and social connections only. Do not use the platform to promote products, businesses, or seek financial support.
  5. Authenticity is Key
    Be yourself. Fake profiles, impersonation, or misleading information are not allowed. Genuine interactions create better connections.
  6. Communicate with Respect
    Treat everyone with kindness. Harassment, hate speech, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.
  7. No Hate
    Discrimination or hate speech based on race, gender, ethnicity, sexuality, religion, or appearance is strictly prohibited. If someone isn’t your type, simply move on respectfully.
  8. Adult Community
    You must be 18 years or older to use GenzMeet. Content involving minors — including childhood photos — is strictly prohibited.
  9. Legal Compliance
    All activities on GenzMeet must comply with local and international laws. Illegal conduct or promotion of banned substances, weapons, or counterfeit goods will result in removal.
  10. Your Space, Your Content
    Only post photos, videos, and text that you own or have permission to use. Respect others’ privacy and intellectual property rights.
  11. Community Integrity
    Do not spread misinformation, spam, or attempt to deceive or manipulate others. Use GenzMeet responsibly and authentically.

Reporting and Enforcement

If you notice behavior or content that violates these guidelines, please report it. Our moderation team reviews every report carefully and takes appropriate actions to maintain safety and integrity across the community.

By following these rules, you help make GenzMeet a respectful, trustworthy, and enjoyable community for everyone. Let’s keep GenzMeet positive and empowering!
