Child Safety Standards (CSAE Policy)

Effective Date: November 2025

At GenzMeet, we are committed to maintaining a safe and responsible environment for all users. We enforce strict policies to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in compliance with Google Play's Child Safety Standards and applicable international laws.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

GenzMeet has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, communication, or behavior involving the abuse, endangerment, grooming, or exploitation of a minor is strictly prohibited and results in immediate account removal and reporting to appropriate authorities.

2. No Minors Allowed

GenzMeet is an adults-only platform. Users must be 18 years or older to create an account or use the service. We do not knowingly allow minors to register, access, or participate on our platform.

3. Prohibited Content & Activities

We strictly prohibit the following:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, sextortion, or solicitation of a minor
  • Trafficking, exploitation, or endangerment of minors
  • Any attempt to misrepresent age in order to contact a minor
