GenzMeet - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

Welcome to GenzMeet. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website and mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, age, gender, and profile details.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions, preferences, and device identifiers.
  • Location Data: If you allow, we may collect approximate location to enhance matches.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar technologies to improve performance and personalize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To show relevant matches and improve recommendations.
  • To communicate updates, offers, or security alerts.
  • To monitor and enhance platform security and performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share limited user information with:

  • Service providers that help operate our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics, email systems).
  • Legal authorities when required by law.
  • Other users, based on your privacy settings and activity within the app.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data anytime.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Request export of your personal information.

To exercise these rights, email us at support@genzmeet.com.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, but no system is completely secure. You use GenzMeet at your own risk.

7. Children’s Privacy

GenzMeet is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us to remove their account.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed outside your country of residence. By using our services, you consent to such transfers in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at genzmeet.com/privacy.html.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@genzmeet.com.
